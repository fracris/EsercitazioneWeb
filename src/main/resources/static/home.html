About Us

Franky's Fishes has been serving the freshest seafood since 1985. Founded by Franky, a passionate fisherman, our shop offers a wide selection of high-quality seafood straight from the ocean.

Leading the Future of Fresh Seafood

Our Journey

Franky started his journey as a fisherman in the 1970s. His dedication to quality and freshness has made Franky's Fishes a trusted name for seafood lovers.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering the best quality seafood. Every product is carefully selected to ensure freshness and flavor.

Current Activities

Today, Franky's Fishes remains a family-run business. We continue to offer fresh seafood daily and are actively involved in sustainable fishing practices to protect our oceans.
